Our Programmes

We lead and empower you, to develop yourself as a unique undergraduate with expectations of the corporate world. Let's grow together!!!

STEP Series

The STEP Series is an event series consisting of three phases that are designed for members of LED KLN to provide opportunities to learn about professional development and elevate a career-oriented mindset to construct the necessary professional foundation

April 14, 2023

Ideathon

LED KLN's 'Inquisitive Minds' is an annual competition that brings some of the greatest undergraduate minds together and provides a vast platform and the opportunity to present their business ideas to provide solutions to real-world scenarios.

April 14, 2023

Business School

Business School is a series of three main phases of events conducted under the guidance of a team of professionals who assist undergraduates in identifying their exciting career paths and provide a series of interviews with corporate organizations for a successful career.

April 14, 2023

LED Talks

LED TALKS is a series of meetings that are organized either to be held online or at university premises throughout the year. These meetings are to be used as the weekly meet-ups for LED KLN members to share knowledge among the inner circle, discuss the progress of the members, and also have fun. It is a meeting series in which members can learn self-development techniques.
