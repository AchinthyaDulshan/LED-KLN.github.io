Avenues of LED KLN

Business Development Avenue

Business Development Avenue was established to offer valuable opportunities for LED KLN members to develop their personalities and make contacts in the corporate industry.

Through this avenue, members can,
Interact with industry professionals.
Increase knowledge of the corporate world.
Form significant partnerships with industry professionals to host events.
Learn how to communicate professionally.

Explore more..
Branding and Marketing Avenue

Branding and Marketing Avenue was established to raise awareness of LED KLN events and memorable moments. This avenue reaches our audience through different social media platforms.

Through this avenue, members can,
Develop their design skills from the ground up.
Learn about various marketing talents.
Learn new strategies for designing and marketing.
Learn about content creation.

Explore more..
Human Resources Avenue

Human Resource Avenue was established to give members experience in member management. This avenue opens up a new space for LED KLN members who are keen on human resource management

Through this avenue, members can,
Be familiar with member management processes.
Assist with the recruiting process within the club.
Improve inter-personal and communication skills.
Learn how to make decisions and solve problems.

Explore more..
Organizational Development Avenue

Organizational Development Avenue was created to help members learn about organizing events and track their progress in the club. The events are planned and outlined to the LED KLN members through this avenue

Through this avenue, members can,
Coordinate with the other avenues.
Assist in carrying out the plans and objectives of the club.
Work in a collaborative way with other avenues.
Learn effective ways of managing tasks.

Explore more..

Message from Senior Treasurer

Message from Senior Treasurer


Seven years ago, a visionary group of students founded LED KLN to bridge the gap between undergraduates and the corporate world. With their intelligence, dedication, and compassionate souls, the members of LED KLN are shining examples of what it means to be a leader. It is both heartening and inspiring to witness their unwavering commitment to making a difference in the world. May LED KLN continue to flourish and stand strong for what it represents and what it has yet to become.
